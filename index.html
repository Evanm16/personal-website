Who I Am

Chasing the story behind the story.

I'm Evan Martinez, an audio journalist and writer based in New York. I produce documentary podcasts, radio features, and long-form reported pieces.

My reporting has taken me from community board meetings to federal courthouses, from rural sound archives to urban noise ordinances. I believe the human voice, recorded well and cut with care, can carry weight that no text ever quite matches.

"Every great piece of audio journalism starts with someone willing to be honest in a room with a recorder." — on craft
